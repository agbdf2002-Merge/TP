# Technology Policy Training Enablement Plan

## Purpose

This plan defines role-based enablement for the published Technology Policy baseline, supporting consistent adoption by architecture, engineering, risk, operations, security, and platform teams.

## Training Objectives

Participants shall understand:

1. The Technology Policy governance hierarchy.
2. The role of standards, principles, patterns, requirements, controls, and evidence.
3. When ARB review is required.
4. How to prepare architecture evidence packs.
5. How exceptions and waivers are submitted, approved, tracked, and remediated.
6. How lifecycle risk is identified and escalated.
7. How ServiceNow, Archer/IRM, ADO/Jira, and GitHub support the operating model.

## Training Tracks

| Track | Audience | Duration | Focus |
|---|---|---:|---|
| Executive Briefing | CIO, senior technology, risk leaders | 30 min | Governance intent, risk posture, adoption metrics |
| Architecture Practitioner | EA, solution, security, platform architects | 90 min | ARB, patterns, standards, evidence packs, decisions |
| Engineering Delivery | Product owners, engineers, scrum leads | 60 min | Intake, design gates, required evidence, delivery traceability |
| Risk and Compliance | Technology Risk, Compliance, Audit partners | 60 min | Requirements, controls, evidence, RTM, exceptions |
| Platform Owner | ServiceNow, Archer/IRM, ADO/Jira owners | 60 min | Field mappings, import templates, sequencing, deferrals |
| Operations and Resilience | Ops, infrastructure, observability, continuity | 60 min | CMDB/CSDM, resilience, backup, observability evidence |

## Core Modules

| Module ID | Module | Required For | Outcome |
|---|---|---|---|
| TRN-001 | Policy hierarchy and governance model | All | Understand operating model and mandatory expectations |
| TRN-002 | ARB decisioning and evidence packs | Architects, engineers, risk | Prepare review-ready submissions |
| TRN-003 | Requirements, controls, and evidence | Architects, risk, control owners | Understand proof chain |
| TRN-004 | Exception and waiver process | All delivery stakeholders | Submit and remediate deviations correctly |
| TRN-005 | Technology lifecycle governance | Architects, ops, app owners | Manage lifecycle risk and EOL/EOS exposure |
| TRN-006 | Tool implementation model | Platform owners, EA, risk | Understand GitHub-to-platform operating model |
| TRN-007 | Metrics and reporting | EA, risk, executives | Operate monthly scorecard and dashboard |

## Enablement Materials

- Executive summary
- ARB checklist
- Architecture evidence pack template
- Exception/waiver guide
- Lifecycle risk guide
- Requirements/control/evidence primer
- ServiceNow / Archer / ADO-Jira implementation overview
- Adoption scorecard walkthrough

## Training Sequence

| Phase | Timing | Activities |
|---|---|---|
| Phase 1 | Day 0-30 | Executive briefing, EA practitioner training, FAQ publication |
| Phase 2 | Day 31-60 | Engineering, risk, compliance, and operations enablement |
| Phase 3 | Day 61-90 | Platform-owner training, scorecard review, evidence operations walkthrough |

## Completion Evidence

Training completion shall be evidenced through:

- attendance logs
- training materials
- Q&A / FAQ updates
- recorded sessions where appropriate
- role-based acknowledgement
- open action register

## Success Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|---|---:|
| Executive briefing completed | 100% of required executive audience |
| EA practitioner training completed | 100% of EA operating team |
| Engineering enablement completed | Initial pilot teams complete by Day 60 |
| Risk/compliance training completed | Required risk partners complete by Day 60 |
| Platform owner briefing completed | Required platform owners complete by Day 90 |
| FAQ published | Day 30 |

## Anti-Patterns

- Training only the EA team and expecting enterprise adoption.
- Confusing awareness sessions with role-based enablement.
- Failing to train control owners on evidence expectations.
- Treating platform-owner deferrals as outside the training scope.
- Not recording questions and decision clarifications.

## Status

Initial training enablement plan established for the Technology Policy post-publication adoption period.
