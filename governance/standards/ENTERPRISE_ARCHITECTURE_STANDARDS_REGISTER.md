# Enterprise Architecture Standards Register

## Purpose

This register defines the initial Enterprise Architecture standards baseline for the Technology Policy framework. Standards implement policy requirements and provide architecture guardrails for delivery teams, product teams, infrastructure teams, security teams, and governance bodies.

## Mandatory Standard Rule

Each Enterprise Architecture standard shall define scope, owner, control intent, mandatory requirements, evidence expectations, exception handling, review cadence, and system of record mapping.

## Review Cadence

Enterprise Architecture standards shall be reviewed annually at minimum and upon material regulatory, risk, technology, platform, merger, or operating model change.
