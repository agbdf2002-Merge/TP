## Release Acceptance Criteria

1. Every volume has an authoritative source file.
2. Every volume has a table of contents and controlled section hierarchy in Word.
3. Requirements and controls use stable, unique identifiers.
4. Regulatory mappings are traceable to requirements, controls, evidence, and accountable owners.
5. Cross-volume references resolve to controlled identifiers.
6. No governance body or decision right is changed through editorial consolidation.
7. Publication derivatives are generated from the approved source baseline.
8. External approval names and dates may remain placeholders until corporate-template finalization.
