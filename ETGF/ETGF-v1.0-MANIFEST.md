# ETGF Version 1.0 Publication Manifest

## Release Position

ETGF Version 1.0 consolidates the Enterprise Technology Policy, enterprise standards, governance engineering artifacts, repository specification, implementation and operating model, assurance package, and executive appendices into seven controlled publication volumes.

## Authoritative Volumes

| Volume | Title | Primary Source Packages |
|---|---|---|
| I | Enterprise Technology Policy | ETP-001 |
| II | Enterprise Standards | ETS-001, ETS-002, ETS-003 |
| III | Governance Engineering | ETGF-04 and associated master registers |
| IV | Repository and Architecture Specification | ETGF-14, ETGF-22, ETGF-26, ETGF-27 |
| V | Implementation and Operating Model | ETGF-06, ETGF-07, ETGF-09, ETGF-12, ETGF-21, ETGF-25 |
| VI | Regulatory, Audit and Assurance | ETGF-08, ETGF-13, ETGF-15, ETGF-17, ETGF-18, ETGF-19, ETGF-24 |
| VII | Executive Appendices, Registers and Templates | ETGF-00, ETGF-05, ETGF-10, ETGF-11, ETGF-16, ETGF-20, ETGF-23 |

## Publication Controls

1. Publication activities shall not modify the approved governance architecture.
2. All final documents shall include a table of contents and controlled section hierarchy.
3. Approval names and effective dates may remain external placeholders until final corporate-template publication.
4. Word output is a publication derivative; source-controlled Markdown and master registers remain the working baseline.
5. Cross-references shall use stable ETGF, ETP, ETS, requirement, control, decision, exception, and evidence identifiers.
