# ETGF Enterprise Glossary

## Purpose

Provide controlled definitions used across ETGF policy, standards, requirements, controls, procedures, evidence, architecture, and implementation artifacts.

## Initial Controlled Terms

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| Architecture Decision Record | A governed record documenting a material architecture decision, rationale, alternatives, impacts, conditions, and approval outcome. |
| Business Application | The logical representation of software supporting one or more business capabilities or services. |
| Business Capability | A stable description of what the Bank must be able to do to execute its strategy and operations. |
| Control | A defined activity or mechanism designed to reduce risk, satisfy a requirement, or provide evidence of compliance. |
| Enterprise Architecture | The discipline that aligns business strategy, capabilities, information, applications, technology, security, and operating models. |
| Evidence | Verifiable information demonstrating that a requirement or control operated as designed during a defined period. |
| Exception | A time-bound, approved deviation from policy, standard, control, pattern, or lifecycle requirement. |
| Policy | A governing statement of management intent and mandatory enterprise direction. |
| Procedure | A defined sequence of activities used to execute policy and standards. |
| Reference Architecture | An approved architecture model defining reusable structures, decisions, controls, and implementation guidance. |
| Requirement | A mandatory statement that must be satisfied and is traceable to policy, standards, risk, regulation, or architecture. |
| Service Offering | A consumable variation of a service with defined commitments, users, and service levels. |
| Standard | A mandatory implementation requirement that operationalizes policy. |
| Technical Service | A technology-facing service delivered by a platform or operations team to support applications and business services. |
| Technology Lifecycle | The governed progression of a technology through emerging, approved, strategic, tolerated, restricted, sunset, and retired states. |

## Maintenance Rule

Terms shall be added, changed, deprecated, or superseded through controlled ETGF publication and shall not be redefined independently within individual volumes.
