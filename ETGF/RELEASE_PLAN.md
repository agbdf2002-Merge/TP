# ETGF v1.0 RC1 Release Plan

## Release Name

ETGF v1.0 RC1

## Release Purpose

Establish the first complete release candidate of the Enterprise Technology Governance Framework for executive review, regulatory readiness, implementation mobilization, and controlled migration into the corporate Word template.

## Authoritative Volumes

1. Volume I – Enterprise Technology Policy
2. Volume II – Enterprise Standards
3. Volume III – Governance Engineering
4. Volume IV – Repository and Architecture Specification
5. Volume V – Implementation and Operating Model
6. Volume VI – Regulatory, Audit and Assurance
7. Volume VII – Executive Appendices, Registers and Templates

## Release Acceptance Criteria

- All artifacts have an accountable owner and lifecycle state.
- Policy, standards, requirements, controls, procedures, evidence, and systems of record are traceable.
- No unresolved identifier collisions remain.
- Word derivatives match the authoritative Markdown baseline.
- Approval names and dates may remain controlled placeholders.
- No governance redesign is introduced during publication.

## Release States

Draft → Internal Review → RC1 → RC2 → Approved → Effective → Superseded

## RC1 Decision Rule

RC1 may be published when content completeness and traceability are sufficient for executive, audit, risk, and regulatory review, even when final corporate formatting and approval dates remain external.
